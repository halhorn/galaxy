@seed
Random seed
RNG seed for initial star positions.
Same seed gives the same layout.
Applied when you restart.

@central_stars_count
Central star count
Number of massive stars at the galactic center.
Must not exceed total disk stars.

@central_stars_mass
Central star mass
Mass of each central star in solar masses (M☉).
Heavier stars dominate gravity near the core.

@disk_stars
Disk stars
Total number of stars, including central stars.
More stars increase compute cost.

@disk_mass_min
Min disk mass
Lower bound for disk star masses (M☉).
Drawn randomly between min and max.

@disk_mass_max
Max disk mass
Upper bound for disk star masses (M☉).
Must be greater than min mass.

@disk_inner_radius
Inner radius
Inner edge of the star disk in AU.
Stars spawn between inner and outer radius.

@disk_outer_radius
Outer radius
Outer edge of the star disk in AU.
Must be greater than inner radius.

@disk_elevation
Disk tilt
Inclination of the disk from the orbital plane (°).
Zero is a flat disk; higher values tilt it in 3D.

@velocity_perturbation
Velocity noise
Random kick added to orbital speeds at startup.
Higher values make less circular orbits.

@softening
Softening
Minimum interaction distance in AU.
Prevents force blow-ups when stars get very close.

@merge_radius_factor
Merge factor
Stars closer than this × combined radius merge into one.
Higher values merge more aggressively.

@force_presets
Force presets
Pre-built force laws: Newtonian gravity, gravity + repulsion, etc.
Select Custom to edit terms manually.

@force_expression
Force expression
Edit individual terms of F = Σ sign · c · d^N.
Add or remove terms; changes apply immediately.

@time_scale
Time scale
Simulation speed multiplier.
1.0 is normal; higher runs faster (keys 1–4 also set this).

@star_visual_scale
Star size
Multiplier for rendered star sphere radius.
Does not affect physics.

@min_star_visual_scale
Min star size
Floor on displayed star size so faint stars stay visible.
Useful when zoomed out.
